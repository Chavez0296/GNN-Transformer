SMALL-WORLD PERTURBATION PROBE ANALYSIS
============================================================

HYPOTHESIS:
Adding random shortcuts should help baseline GNNs more than
transformers, because baseline GNNs rely on edge structure
while transformers already have global attention.

RESULTS:
  Baseline (no shortcuts): F1 = 0.5649
  Transformer (no shortcuts): F1 = 0.5232

  At 0.1 shortcuts/node:
    Baseline improvement: -0.0857
    Transformer improvement: -0.0064
    Difference (baseline - transformer): -0.0793

  At 0.2 shortcuts/node:
    Baseline improvement: +0.0018
    Transformer improvement: +0.0038
    Difference (baseline - transformer): -0.0020

CONCLUSION:
  HYPOTHESIS NOT SUPPORTED: Transformer improves more (or equally) from shortcuts.
  This may indicate that shortcuts provide additional useful structure
  that benefits both architectures equally.
