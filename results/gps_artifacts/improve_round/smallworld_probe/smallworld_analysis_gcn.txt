SMALL-WORLD PERTURBATION PROBE ANALYSIS
============================================================

HYPOTHESIS:
Adding random shortcuts should help baseline GNNs more than
transformers, because baseline GNNs rely on edge structure
while transformers already have global attention.

RESULTS:
  Baseline (no shortcuts): F1 = 0.5042
  Transformer (no shortcuts): F1 = 0.5290

  At 0.1 shortcuts/node:
    Baseline improvement: +0.0507
    Transformer improvement: -0.0343
    Difference (baseline - transformer): +0.0850

  At 0.2 shortcuts/node:
    Baseline improvement: +0.0299
    Transformer improvement: -0.0026
    Difference (baseline - transformer): +0.0326

CONCLUSION:
  HYPOTHESIS SUPPORTED: Baseline improves more from shortcuts than transformer.
  This suggests baseline benefits from structural shortcuts because it lacks
  the global attention that transformers have built-in.
